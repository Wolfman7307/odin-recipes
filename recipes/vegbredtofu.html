Vegan Breaded Tofu

Description

Easy and tasty breaded tofu recipe. A vegan main dish served with brown rice. It's delicious! I use sprouted tofu, it's better for digestion!

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 125 calories; protein 5.1g; carbohydrates 5.8g; fat 9.6g; sodium 70.1mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1/4 Cup Vegan Mayonnaise
  • 2 Tablespoons Water
  • 1/4 Cup Bread Crumbs, As Needed
  • 12 Ounce Package Tofu, Sliced Into 1/2-Inch Strips
